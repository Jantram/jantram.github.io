Dr. Ritesh Sarkhel

Assistant Professor, Department of Computer Science & Engineering

Dr. Ritesh Sarkhel leads the Jantram Research Group at IIT Kharagpur. The lab's primary mission focuses on building next-generation Agentic AI frameworks, advanced Large Language Model architectures, and structured Reasoning workflows capable of making deep inferences over complex real-world documentation.
